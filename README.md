🌱 # Leafistry: A Plant Lover's Haven
Leafistry is a user-friendly web application that brings the joy of plants to your fingertips. Whether you're a seasoned green thumb or just starting your plant journey, Leafistry offers everything you need—from discovering the perfect plant for your lifestyle to shopping for greenery and learning plant care tips in our library.

## Features
### Shop Plants and Accessories
Browse through a curated selection of indoor plants and accessories tailored to various needs and preferences.

### Discover Your Perfect Match
Take our fun quiz to find plants that align with your lifestyle, space, and care preferences.

### Comprehensive Plant Library
Explore detailed plant information, including care requirements, growth habits, and aesthetic purposes.

### Engaging Blogs
Stay informed with articles about plant care, indoor gardening trends, and expert tips.

### Personalized Recommendations
Get tailored plant suggestions based on your preferences and environment.
